Victor Wooten

Marcus Miller


Marcus Miller is a highly accomplished American bassist, composer, and producer. He is renowned for his virtuosic bass playing skills and his contributions to the jazz-fusion genre. With a prolific career spanning several decades, Miller has collaborated with numerous legendary musicians and released a string of critically acclaimed albums.

Flea


Flea, whose real name is Michael Peter Balzary, is an iconic American musician and bassist. He is best known as a founding member and bassist of the influential rock band Red Hot Chili Peppers. Flea's energetic and unique playing style, as well as his distinctive stage presence, have made him one of the most recognizable and respected bassists in the music industry.
